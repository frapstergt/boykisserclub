Monkeverse Privacy Policy: 1. No user data is stored on our game servers, the only data we access
is your approx. location from your ip from playfab,
we dont use this data for any purposes, though.

2. The only data we do keep track of is your in-game statistics,
this is to keep track of stuff such as your in game cosmetics,
preferences, etc.

3. We do not store trackable device info, only your
headset's unique identifier to keep track of your
playerID for playfab.

4. We do not store any voice/chat communications inside nor
outside of the game.
